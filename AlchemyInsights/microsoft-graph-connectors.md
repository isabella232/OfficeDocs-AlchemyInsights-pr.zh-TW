- [ Microsoft 連接器](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoftsearch/connectors-gallery#Microsoft)

如需詳細資訊，請參閱[Microsoft Graph 連接器一覽](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoftsearch/connectors-overview)。
