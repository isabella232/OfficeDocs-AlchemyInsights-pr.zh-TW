# <a name="inventory-in-microsoft-365-apps-admin-center"></a>Microsoft 365 Apps 系統管理中心的庫存

- [遺失或重複的裝置](https://docs.microsoft.com/deployoffice/admincenter/inventory#missing-or-duplicate-devices)
